// About the Project

What is NERV Central?

NERV Central is a fan-created reference archive dedicated to Neon Genesis Evangelion — the 1995 animated series directed by Hideaki Anno and produced by Gainax. This site is intended as a clean, readable resource for both first-time viewers and longtime fans who want a quick reference on the series' characters, episodes, and lore. Why Evangelion?

Neon Genesis Evangelion is widely considered one of the most influential works in animation history. It fundamentally changed how anime approached psychological complexity, unreliable narration, and introspective storytelling. Its themes — isolation, depression, identity, connection, and the cost of existing — remain deeply resonant decades after its original broadcast.

// Evangelion Pilots (The Children)

Shinji Ikari

Third Child — Unit-01 Pilot

Age14
Sync RatePeak 400%+
UnitEvangelion Unit-01
StatusActive

The reluctant protagonist of the series. Shinji was abandoned by his father Gendo at a young age and struggles with extreme self-worth issues. Despite his reluctance, he becomes one of NERV's most effective pilots. His internal conflict — between the need for human connection and the fear of rejection — drives much of the series' psychological depth.

Rei Ayanami

First Child — Unit-00 Pilot

Age14
Sync RateClassified
UnitEvangelion Unit-00
StatusMultiple Instances

Quiet, detached, and deeply mysterious, Rei is the First Child and the first pilot recruited by NERV. Her origins are shrouded in secrecy and her connection to Yui Ikari and Lilith raises disturbing questions about her very nature. She shows little emotion but gradually demonstrates her capacity for sacrifice.

Asuka Langley Soryu

Second Child — Unit-02 Pilot

Age14
Sync RatePeak ~300%
UnitEvangelion Unit-02
StatusActive

Fiercely proud and fiercely competitive, Asuka is the Second Child, raised as a prodigy pilot in Germany. Her bravado conceals deep psychological trauma rooted in her mother's breakdown and suicide. She views piloting as her entire identity and struggles greatly when that identity is threatened.

Toji Suzuhara

Fourth Child — Unit-03 Pilot

Age14
Sync RateUnknown
UnitEvangelion Unit-03
StatusRetired (Injured)

A classmate of Shinji's and a straightforward, honest young man. Toji agrees to pilot Unit-03 to secure medical treatment for his hospitalized sister. His activation test ends in catastrophe during the "Dummy System" incident, leaving him permanently injured.

Kaworu Nagisa

Fifth Child — 17th Angel (Tabris)

AgeAppears 15
Sync RateAbsolute
UnitEvangelion Unit-02
True Form17th Angel — Tabris

The final Angel and the most enigmatic character in the series. Kaworu arrives at NERV as the Fifth Child and immediately forms a profound bond with Shinji — offering him the unconditional acceptance Shinji has always craved. His warmth and empathy are genuine, even as his mission brings him into direct conflict with humanity's survival. His final moments with Shinji remain among the most affecting in all of anime.

// NERV Command Staff

Gendo Ikari

Supreme Commander, NERV

Age48
ClearanceOmega-Level
AllegiancePersonal Agenda

Cold, calculating, and utterly inscrutable, Gendo Ikari is the Supreme Commander of NERV and Shinji's estranged father. He orchestrates events behind the scenes in service of his own version of the Human Instrumentality Project — motivated by his desire to reunite with his deceased wife, Yui.

Misato Katsuragi

Major / Operations Director

Age29
ClearanceClass-B
AllegianceNERV / Pilots

NERV's operations director and the guardian of Shinji and later Asuka. Misato survived the Second Impact as a child and has spent her life driven by a need for revenge against the Angels. Beneath her casual, messy exterior is a deeply wounded and fiercely protective person.

Ritsuko Akagi

Chief Scientist, Project E

Age30
ClearanceClass-A
AllegianceNERV / Science

NERV's head scientist and the lead developer of the Magi supercomputer system. Ritsuko is brilliant and pragmatic, though her loyalty to Gendo — and the secrets she keeps on his behalf — gradually corrode her integrity. Her mother, Naoko Akagi, built the Magi before her.
